About Our Hotel

Hotel Vlachogiannis is a family-owned hotel located just 200 meters from Skala Prinos Beach in Thassos. It offers comfortable family rooms with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and a beautiful terrace surrounded by a garden. Our guests can also enjoy free parking and a welcoming atmosphere perfect for a relaxing stay.
